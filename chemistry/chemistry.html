What is Chemistry?

Chemistry in Simple Terms

Chemistry is the study of stuff and how stuff changes. It's all about matter (anything that takes up space and has weight) and what happens when matter interacts with other matter.

Why Study Chemistry?

Chemistry is everywhere in your daily life! From cooking food and cleaning products to breathing and medicine, understanding chemistry helps you make sense of the world around you.

Basic Concepts

You'll explore matter, states of matter (solids, liquids, gases), and energy. These fundamental concepts will help you understand more complex chemical reactions and processes.
