Contribution Process

1

Get Started

Create an account on our platform and join our Discord community. Introduce yourself and let us know how you'd like to contribute.

2

Browse our list of open issues and projects. Find something that matches your skills and interests, or propose your own idea.

Reach out to our team or other contributors working on similar projects. Collaboration is key to creating great learning experiences.
